CHAT ITEMS

Pani Poori

One of the most famous chats among people of all ages. Having this stall in the wedding can bring more cheer to the wedding itself. One can have a ‘Gol Gappa’ event!

Mumbai ragda patties

A signature street food of Mumbai not to be missed in any North Indian wedding, made with zero no numkeen, chatne. Make sure a stall dedicated for this epic chat!

Mini Samosa

Mini Samosa’s are an all-time favorite for people belonging to various age groups. Make sure you have this dish in your wedding as most people would have the appetite for it!

Dahi Puri

This awesome dish is known for its crispy texture & enticing taste, made with the Dahi, namkeen, red chatne. A must-have for your wedding!

Cocktail Kachori

These deep fried crispy & crunchy balls of maida flour is a scrumptious snack that is a must-have for your wedding!

Dhokla

This exquisite dish from Gujarat can be served as a main course or a side dish or as a snack. There are several types of Dhokla’s that could decorate your wedding spread.
